About Me

Dr. Nilesh K. Modi

I am Dr. Nilesh Modi, a passionate researcher and academician with over 12 years of experience in academics and the IT industry. My vision is to elevate academic institutions to thrive in a competitive global environment while fostering innovation and excellence.

I hold a Doctorate in E-Security (Computer Science and Applications) and am actively pursuing post-doctoral research in Wireless Communication and Security, alongside certification in Ethical Hacking. My primary research focus is on Cyber Security, where I aim to develop advanced solutions to address modern challenges in the digital landscape.

Professional Engagements:
I am an active life member of professional organizations such as CSI, IEEE, IACSIT, IACSI, and IAEng. As a consultant, I contribute to IT system development projects and have successfully implemented various government-funded initiatives. I serve as a member of the Board of Studies and Selection Committees at Gujarat Technological University and several other universities in India.

Leadership Roles:
Currently, I am working as the District Coordinator for the SANDHAN Program, a significant initiative by the Government of Gujarat aimed at enhancing educational standards through modern technology. My commitment to academia, research, and industry drives me to make impactful contributions that align with national and global advancements.
